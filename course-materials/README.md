# Course Materials

This folder contains supporting materials used in Applied Business Statistics (BUSN 5760).

The contents include assignments, study guides, practice problems, reference materials, statistical tables, slides, and other instructional resources used throughout the course.

## Contents

**syllabus**  
Course syllabus.

**homework-assignments**  
Assignments used in the course.

**study-guides**  
Exam preparation guides.

**practice-problems**  
Additional exercises covering descriptive statistics, probability, and hypothesis testing.

**reference**  
Supplementary materials including articles, cheat sheets, and statistical tables.

**excel**  
Excel exercises and demonstrations used in class.

**other-slides**  
Additional lecture slides for topics such as causality and econometrics.

**funny**  
A few statistical jokes and visual examples occasionally used in class.
